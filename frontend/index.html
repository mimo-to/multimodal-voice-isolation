How does this work?

This system solves the Cocktail Party Problem: isolating a single voice from a mix of overlapping speakers.

  1. Mix: Audio from both videos is blended into a single noisy signal.
  2. Separate: A neural network (Conv-TasNet) splits the mix into two isolated voice tracks.
  3. Track lips: MediaPipe detects facial landmarks in the target video and measures lip movement over time.
  4. Match: The system compares each separated track's vocal energy rhythm against the lip movement pattern. The track with the highest correlation is identified as the target speaker.
